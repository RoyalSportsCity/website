THE NEXT STEP

Our mission is to expand the usage and availability of services built using blockchain technology, and to apply those services to cities, towns and the lives of citizens. We want to make blockchain technology available for everyone to use. And to do just that we are building the Royal Sports City Chain blockchain and the Royal Sports City Platform.

Blockchain technology is not a magic bullet that can be applied to every exisiting computer or human system. That is why we are building the Royal Sports City Platform on top of the Royal Sports City Chain blockchain, ensuring that we are utilizing the most optimal technology for the different services and experiences provided by Royal Sports City Chain and the Royal Sports City Platform.

Cities and citizens will be able to use the Royal Sports City Chain to improve how they communicate, socialize, trade, and manage assets (both physical and digital). In addition, it will be possible to reduce costs and overhead, while simultaneously empowering us with all the tools we need to take the next step forward!
